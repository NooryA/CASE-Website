About Us


Community Alliance for Support and Empowerment (C.A.S.E.) is a not-for-profit and grassroots community organization located in Region of Peel.


Our vision: To be a leading community agency in empowering the marginalized population to positvely influence their communities.


MANDATE: To empower the marginalized who face multiple barriers overcome those barriers by accessing support and services they need to achieve their full potential and enhance their quality of life
